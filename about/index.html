About N&R AI Solutions

Two people who believe powerful technology should be accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Why We Started This

We started N&R AI Solutions because we kept seeing the same pattern: incredible AI tools becoming available to anyone with an internet connection — but only truly useful to people who already knew how to set them up. Everyone else was left with something that either felt confusing, didn't quite work, or required constant tinkering to maintain.

We're based in Florida and we built this business for the people who were being left behind — the small business owner who doesn't have an IT department, the retiree who wants to simplify their life, the busy parent who just needs something to actually help. People who have real things to do and don't want to spend their time learning software.

Our approach is simple: we listen to what you actually need, build something that genuinely fits your life, and stay with you after it's set up. No jargon, no complicated dashboards, no guesswork on your end.

How We Work

Our Approach

Three things we hold to with every client, every time.

We Speak Human, Not Tech

We explain things in plain language. You'll never get an email full of acronyms or jargon. If something technical comes up, we translate it.

We Only Set Up What You've Approved

Nothing gets connected or configured without your explicit sign-off. We walk through every access request with you before making any changes.

We Stay With You After Launch

Setup isn't the end — it's the beginning. Ongoing support is included. If something needs adjusting or you want to add something new, just reach out.
