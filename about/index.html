The Team

About Otian AI

Two people who believe powerful technology should be accessible to everyone.

Otian is drawn from the Latin otium - the ease that comes when someone trusted is quietly handling your affairs for you.

Our Story

Why We Started This

We started Otian AI because we kept seeing the same pattern: powerful AI tools available to anyone, but only truly useful to people who already knew how to set them up. Everyone else was left with something confusing, half-working, or requiring constant tinkering.

Jett Nguyen and Jack Raney met as co-workers at MARA Holdings Inc. (Jett in Florida, Jack in Texas), and realized we made a strong team: one of us sees where AI can genuinely help people, the other builds and implements it. Otian AI grew out of that partnership.

We work fully remotely with clients nationwide, over Zoom. Our approach is simple: listen carefully, teach you as we build something that genuinely fits your life, and stay with you after it's set up - no jargon, no complicated dashboards, no guesswork.

How We Work

Our Approach

Three things we hold to with every client, every time.

We Speak Human, Not Tech

We explain things in plain language. You'll never get an email full of acronyms or jargon. If something technical comes up, we translate it.

We Only Set Up What You've Approved

Nothing gets connected or configured without your explicit sign-off. We walk through every access request with you before making any changes.

We Stay With You After Launch

Setup is not the end. It is the beginning. Ongoing support is included. If something needs adjusting or you want to add something new, just reach out.

Want to work with us?

Fill out our intake form and we'll reach out within one business day to talk through what you need.
