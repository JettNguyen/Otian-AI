The Idea

Your Own Agent, Living on Your Computer

Archie is a desktop app that runs your own AI agent, right on your computer. You talk to it the way you’d text a friend, and it actually gets things done — keeping your calendar straight, staying on top of your inbox, running research, and handling the busywork you’d rather not.

What makes it different is where it lives. Your agent, its memory, and its keys live on your machine — not on someone else’s servers. You’re not renting a sliver of a company’s cloud. You own the whole thing.

What You Can Build

Shape It Piece by Piece

Archie is modular. You start with a blank agent and build it up with the pieces you want — so no two agents are ever quite the same.

Personality

How your agent talks to you. Warm and chatty or brief and to the point — you set the voice and the vibe.

Skills

The things it can do. Add new abilities in a click from the built-in marketplace — calendar management, inbox triage, and more.

Workers

Specialists your agent can hand work to — a researcher, a writer — each focused on doing one thing well.

Routines

Things it does on its own, on a schedule. A briefing every morning, a report every Friday — without being asked.

Knowledge Base

Give it your files — notes, PDFs, documents — so it answers from what you know, not just what it was trained on.

Connections

Link the tools you already use, like Gmail and Google Calendar — only ever when you say so.

Yours Alone

Your Keys Never Leave Your Computer

Your agent runs on your own computer. Its instructions, your files, its memory, and your keys are stored on your device — the keys in your system’s own keychain, which we have no way to read. It talks to the AI through your own account, so your conversations never pass through us, and we keep no copy of them.

What lives on your computer

  • Your agent and everything it knows
  • Your files, notes, and its memory
  • Your connected accounts and API keys
  • Every message you exchange

What Otian can see

  • Your email address, so you can sign in
  • Whether your subscription is active
  • Which paid add-ons you’ve bought
  • Never your chats, files, or accounts
  • No central copy of your data to breach
No Setup Headaches

Built for People, Not Programmers

Running your own AI agent used to mean terminals, config files, and copying secret keys between windows. Archie tucks all of that behind a friendly app. If you can send a text and click a button, you can run it — and when you’d like a hand getting started, our guided setup is one message away.
