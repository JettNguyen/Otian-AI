Meet Archie

The desktop app we’re building to put a personal AI assistant on your own computer — one you shape, name, and truly own. No terminals, no code, nothing leaving your Mac.

The Idea

Your Own Assistant, Living on Your Mac

Archie is a Mac app that runs your own AI assistant, right on your computer. You talk to it the way you’d text a friend, and it actually gets things done — keeping your calendar straight, staying on top of your inbox, running research, and handling the busywork you’d rather not.

What makes it different is where it lives. Your assistant, its memory, and everything it touches stay on your machine — not on someone else’s servers. You’re not renting a sliver of a company’s cloud. You own the whole thing.

What You Can Build

Shape It Piece by Piece

Archie is modular. You start with a blank assistant and build it up with the pieces you want — so no two assistants are ever quite the same.

Personality

How your assistant talks to you. Warm and chatty or brief and to the point — you set the voice and the vibe.

Skills & Add-ons

The things it can do. Add new abilities in a click from the built-in marketplace — calendar management, inbox triage, and more.

Workers

Specialists your assistant can hand work to — a researcher, a writer — each focused on doing one thing well.

Routines

Things it does on its own, on a schedule. A briefing every morning, a report every Friday — without being asked.

Knowledge Base

Give it your files — notes, PDFs, documents — so it answers from what you know, not just what it was trained on.

Connections

Link the tools you already use, like Gmail and Google Calendar — only ever when you say so.

Yours Alone

Everything Stays on Your Computer

Your assistant runs entirely on your Mac. Its instructions, your files, your chat history, and your keys stay on your device — kept in your Mac’s secure storage, never on our servers. It talks to the AI through your own account, so your conversations never pass through us.

What lives on your Mac

  • Your assistant and everything it knows
  • Your files, notes, and its memory
  • Your connected accounts and API keys
  • Every message you exchange

What Otian can see

  • Only whether your subscription is active
  • Never your chats, files, or accounts
  • No central copy of your data to breach
  • You can pull the plug anytime
No Setup Headaches

Built for People, Not Programmers

Running your own AI assistant used to mean terminals, config files, and copying secret keys between windows. Archie tucks all of that behind a friendly app. If you can send a text and click a button, you can run it — and when you’d like a hand getting started, our guided setup is one message away.
