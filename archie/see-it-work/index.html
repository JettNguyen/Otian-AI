A morning briefing you didn’t ask for twice

This one runs a Daily Briefing routine: every morning it works out what matters, checks the calendar, reads the markets, and sends the whole thing over Telegram before its owner is properly awake.

Nobody typed a command. The routine was installed once, and it has been showing up every day since — which is the entire point. An agent you have to remember to use is just another app.

Everything it did happened on its owner’s own computer, on their own accounts. We never saw a word of it.
