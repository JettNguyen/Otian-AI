Investment

Built Around Your Business, Priced the Same Way

No two businesses need the same thing - some owners want a simple assistant for customer replies and scheduling, others need one that tracks leads, follows up with contacts, and integrates with multiple tools across several employees. A flat rate would leave one overpaying and the other underserved, so our quotes factor in workflow complexity, how many tools need connecting, and how many people on your team need their own access.

The AI model itself (Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini) runs on its own small subscription, typically around $20 a month per active user billed directly by the provider, separate from our fee - we'll help you choose the right one and walk you through signing up.
