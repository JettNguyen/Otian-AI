Yes, and it’s small and separate from our service fee — though how it works depends on which path you’re on. If we’re setting up your agent together through our guided service, you’ll want a standard AI subscription (like Claude Pro or ChatGPT Plus, usually around $20 per month) to work with during the build. If you’re using the Archie app, it connects to the AI through your own API key instead — a pay-as-you-go account where you only pay for what your agent actually does. Each message costs anywhere from a fraction of a cent to a few cents, so a typical personal agent runs just a few dollars a month. Work it hard — long research, big documents, all-day back-and-forth — and it climbs into the tens of dollars, still a fraction of what a person doing the same job would cost. You add a little credit up front (say $5–$20), it draws down slowly, and you can set a hard spending cap on the provider’s website. Inside Archie you can also dial each agent between Economy, Balanced, and Best models, so you decide the balance of cost and quality.
They’re two different products from the same company. A Claude or ChatGPT subscription only works on that company’s own website and app — it can’t be used by other programs. Your Archie agent runs on your own computer, so it connects to the AI through a separate doorway called an API, which bills on its own based on actual use. The upside: for most people the API costs less than a monthly subscription, because you only pay for the messages your agent actually sends — often just a few dollars a month — and you stay in full control of the account and the spending. Your existing Claude Pro or ChatGPT Plus plan keeps working as normal; the API is simply what lets a program you own talk to the AI on your behalf.
An API key is like a password that lets your agent use the AI on your behalf. Because your agent lives on your computer — not on our servers — it talks to the AI company directly, with your key. That means you always own the account, you see exactly what’s spent, and your conversations never pass through us. Getting a key takes about two minutes, and the app walks you through every step in plain language.
