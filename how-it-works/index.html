Your Path to a Running Agent

Five steps from a quiet computer to an agent you can text from anywhere.

01

Pick a Computer It Can Live On

Any computer you can leave on will do — the one you already own probably qualifies. Here’s what you need.

Install Archie

Download the desktop app and open it. Archie is in testing — see where it stands today.

02
03

Build Your Agent

Name it, then shape it with add-ons: Skills, Workers, Routines, and Personalities. Start with the free built-ins, and browse the marketplace when you want more.

Connect Your Chat App

Link your agent to Telegram, Discord, or Slack — whichever you already use.

04
05

Text It From Anywhere

Your agent does the work on your machine and replies in your chat. It sends email and updates your calendar only after you approve each one — it proposes, you approve. It can’t spend money, though; that ability doesn’t exist in Archie at all.

Hit a Ceiling? Tell Us

If Archie can’t do it yet, we’ll build the add-on. Every commissioned add-on becomes a marketplace listing — your request becomes everyone’s feature.

06

On Your Own, or With Us Beside You

You can do all of this yourself. Guided setup is for everyone who’d rather not do it alone.

Do It Yourself

  • Install Archie and build your agent at your own pace
  • Start with the free built-in add-ons; paid ones are a one-time purchase
  • Connect only the accounts you choose — your keys stay on your machine, where we can’t read them
  • Hit a ceiling? Tell us — we’ll build the add-on

Take the Guided Path

  • A free discovery call to map out what you want
  • We build with you over screen share, teaching every step in plain language
  • Bigger setups can take more than one session — we tell you which on that free call
  • Archie is a one-time license, separate from your sessions
  • Read more about guided setup
