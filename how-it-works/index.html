Step by Step

Your Path to a Running Assistant

Six straightforward steps to build your assistant with the level of guidance that fits you.

01

Fill Out Our Short Intake Form

Takes about five minutes, no tech jargon required. Tell us about your day-to-day tasks, your comfort level, and how hands-on you'd like to be.

We Review Your Submission

We review your answers carefully, and if it's a good fit, reach out within one business day to move forward - making sure we can genuinely help before either of us invests more time.

02
03

Discovery Call on Zoom — Completely Free

A completely free Zoom call to talk through your needs, answer questions, and agree on how we'll work together - meeting frequency, check-in times, all over screen share so you see exactly what's happening.

We Guide the Build and Configuration

We build your assistant together on your computer, connecting only the accounts you approve, with Zoom guidance at your pace.

04
05

You Try It. We Fine-Tune.

You use your assistant for real tasks and give us feedback - completely normal. Every setup gets a tuning period where we adjust the details until it works exactly how you want.

Your Assistant Runs From Here On

Once you're happy, it runs from your machine - ongoing support stays available, from light check-ins to hands-on help as you expand capabilities.

Roles

What to Expect

We've designed the process so you can choose your level of involvement without getting overwhelmed.

What You'll Need to Do

  • Fill out our intake form (about 5 minutes)
  • Join a completely free Zoom discovery call once we've reviewed your submission
  • Choose your preferred guidance level (hands-on, hands-off, or hybrid)
  • Give feedback during the fine-tuning period
  • Message your assistant when you need something

How We Support You

  • Reviewing your intake and confirming it's a good fit
  • Guided setup and configuration on your own computer
  • Choosing and configuring the right AI model
  • Walking through every account connection over Zoom
  • Testing and quality-checking before launch
  • Fine-tuning based on your early feedback
  • Ongoing coaching and troubleshooting after launch
