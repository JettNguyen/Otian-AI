The Process

How It Works

From your first question to a running AI assistant. Here is the full picture.

Step by Step

Your Path to a Running Assistant

Six straightforward steps. Most of which we handle entirely on our end.

01

Fill Out Our Short Intake Form

Takes about five minutes. You tell us what you'd like help with. No tech jargon required. We'll ask about your day-to-day tasks, your comfort level, and how hands-on you'd like to be.

We Review Your Submission

We go through your answers carefully. If it looks like a good fit, we'll reach out within one business day to accept and move forward. This step ensures we can genuinely help before either of us invests more time.

02
03

Discovery Call on Zoom

We schedule a Zoom call to talk through your needs in detail, answer any questions, and agree on how we'll work together, including meeting frequency and the best times to check in. Everything is done via screen share so you can see exactly what's happening.

We Build and Configure Your Assistant

We set everything up around your specific needs and connect only the accounts you've approved, walking you through each one over Zoom so nothing happens without your explicit sign-off.

04
05

You Try It. We Fine-Tune.

You use your assistant for real tasks and give us feedback. This is normal. Every setup gets a tuning period where we adjust the details until it works exactly the way you want it to.

Your Assistant Runs From Here On

Once you're happy, your assistant works quietly in the background. Ongoing support is included. If anything needs adjusting or you want to add a new capability, just reach out.

What to Expect

We've intentionally designed the process so your involvement is minimal and stress-free.

What You'll Need to Do

  • Fill out our intake form (about 5 minutes)
  • Join a Zoom discovery call once we've reviewed your submission
  • Agree on meeting times and check-in frequency during that call
  • Give feedback during the fine-tuning period
  • Message your assistant when you need something

What We Handle

  • Reviewing your intake and confirming it's a good fit
  • Everything related to setup and configuration
  • Choosing and configuring the right AI model
  • Walking through every account connection over Zoom
  • Testing and quality-checking before launch
  • Fine-tuning based on your early feedback
  • Ongoing support after your assistant is live
