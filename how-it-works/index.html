Step by Step

Your Path to a Running Agent

Five steps from a quiet computer to an agent you can text from anywhere — and one promise for whatever comes after.

01

Pick a Computer It Can Live On

The only real requirement is a computer you can leave on. A Mac mini makes an ideal home, but it’s never mandatory — the machine you already own probably qualifies. Here’s what you need.

Install Archie

Download the desktop app and open it. Archie is in development — see where it stands today.

02
03

Build Your Agent

Name it, then shape it with add-ons: Skills, Workers, Routines, and Personalities. Start with the free built-ins, and browse the marketplace when you want more.

Connect Your Chat App

Link your agent to Telegram, Discord, or Slack — whichever you already use. From then on, talking to it is just texting.

04
05

Text It From Anywhere

Your agent does the work on your machine and replies in your chat. It can’t send email and can’t spend money — those abilities don’t exist in Archie, so there is nothing to switch off.

Hit a Ceiling? Tell Us

If Archie can’t do it yet, tell us — we’ll build the add-on. Every commissioned add-on becomes a marketplace listing, so your one-off request becomes everyone’s feature.

06
Two Ways In

On Your Own, or With Us Beside You

Archie is the product, and it’s built so you can do all of this yourself. Guided setup is the door for everyone who’d rather not walk through it alone.

Do It Yourself

  • Install Archie and build your agent at your own pace
  • Start with the free built-in add-ons; paid ones are a one-time purchase
  • Connect only the accounts you choose — your keys stay on your machine, where we can’t read them
  • When you hit something Archie can’t do yet, tell us — we’ll build the add-on

Take the Guided Path

  • A completely free first call to map out what you want
  • We build with you over screen share, teaching every step in plain language
  • Bigger setups can take more than one session — we tell you which on that free call
  • Archie is free while you’re an active client
  • Read more about guided setup
