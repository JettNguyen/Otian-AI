What We Do

What is a Personal AI Agent?

Think of it as a remote assistant powered by AI. Message it tasks — “reply to this client,” “find me a flight,” “sort these files” — and it gets to work, then checks in when needed.

Always in Your Control

Archie sends email and changes your calendar only after you approve each action — it proposes, you approve, then it acts. It can’t spend money, though — that ability doesn’t exist in the app. It speaks only in the chats you connect it to, to people you’ve approved. The full, honest list is on our Trust page.

Works Around the Clock

Your agent never sleeps — it handles tasks in the background while you’re busy or asleep, then fills you in.

No Tech Experience Needed

No terminal, no code. If you can install an app and send a message, you can run an agent.

The Modular System

One Agent, Shaped by Add-ons

Every agent starts simple and grows with add-ons — pieces you browse and install from the built-in marketplace, in a click. They come in four kinds.

Skills

The things your agent can do. Inbox triage, calendar management, file cleanup — each one a new ability.

Workers

Specialists your agent hands work to — a researcher, a writer — each focused on doing one thing well.

Routines

Things it does on its own, on a schedule. A briefing every morning, a report every Friday — without being asked.

Personalities

How your agent talks to you. Warm and chatty or brief and to the point — you set the voice and the vibe.

And if Archie can’t do it yet, tell us — we’ll build the add-on.

Every add-on we build for one person joins the marketplace for everyone. That’s what “no ceiling” means.

Your Machine, Your Accounts

We Never Hold Your Data

Your agent lives on your own computer, under your own accounts. Its instructions, your files, and everything it remembers sit on your hard drive — and your key is kept in your Mac’s Keychain, where we have no way to read it. When it needs to think, it talks to the AI company directly, on your account. We are never in the middle of that, and we keep no copy of it.

Typical Cloud AI

You hand a company your accounts, your messages, and your files — and they keep them. That data sits on their servers indefinitely: a standing target for a breach, and an asset on someone else’s balance sheet, no matter how careful they are.

Your Archie Agent

It runs on the computer in your home or office, under your accounts, with your keys. We store none of it — so there is no Otian archive of your life to be breached, subpoenaed, or sold. You decide what it can touch, and you can pull the plug anytime.

Our servers know three things about you: your email address, whether you own Archie, and which paid add-ons you’ve bought. Not your emails, not your calendar, not a single conversation. Free add-ons we can’t see at all.
