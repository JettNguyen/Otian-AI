What It’s Like to Have Your Own Agent

One agent, on your own computer, quietly working through the inbox, the calendar, the errands, and the research you never get to.

The Small Stuff, Handled

Your agent takes the repetitive work off your plate. Think of it as a remote assistant powered by AI: it drafts, sorts, and reminds — email goes out only when you tap Send, and calendar changes wait for your approval.

Email & InboxFlags priority messages and proposes ready-to-send replies
Scheduling & RemindersSuggests best slots and drafts confirmation messages
Research & LookupsBuilds ranked lists and summarizes options with next steps
Lists & TrackingKeeps running lists — follow-ups, applications, plans — and answers questions about them
Following Up with ContactsPrepares personalized check-ins using recent context
Daily BriefingsSurfaces your schedule, priority emails, and key reminders each morning
