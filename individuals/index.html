Investment

Built Around You, Priced the Same Way

No two clients need the same thing. Some people want a simple assistant that filters their inbox and sends them a daily summary. Others need something more comprehensive: one that handles customer inquiries, tracks bills, follows up with contacts, and integrates with multiple apps. Building both for the same flat rate would mean one person pays too much and the other pays too little.

Our quotes account for workflow complexity, how many tools need to be connected, and how much guidance you want from us while building and learning the system.

The AI model itself (Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini) comes with its own small monthly subscription, typically around $20 per month, billed directly by the provider. That is separate from our fee. We'll help you choose the right one and walk you through signing up.
