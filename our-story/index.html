Our Story

It Started With One Agent

We did the hard part by hand, over and over, until we could do it in software. That method is the company.

Otian is drawn from the Latin otium — the ease that comes when someone trusted is quietly handling your affairs for you.

How We Got Here

By Hand, One at a Time

Jett set up an AI agent for Jack. We used OpenClaw, because it was secure and endlessly customizable — the two things we actually cared about. It was also miserable to set up.

So we started doing it for other people. By hand, one at a time. And doing it by hand, over and over, taught us exactly where the friction lived — which steps tripped people up, which ones needed an hour of explaining, and which ones nobody should ever have to see.

Archie is what we learned. Same principles — secure, yours, no ceiling — without the terminal. It’s inspired by OpenClaw, not built on it.

Now we’re about to do the same thing again, with add-ons. The method is deliberate: do the hard part by hand until you can do it in software. If Archie can’t do it yet, tell us — we’ll build the add-on. And every add-on we build for one person becomes a marketplace listing for everyone, so one-off work compounds into product.

Jett Nguyen and Jack Raney met as co-workers at MARA Holdings Inc. — Jett in Florida, Jack in Texas — and realized we made a strong team: one of us sees where AI can genuinely help people, the other builds and implements it. We work fully remotely with clients nationwide, over Zoom. When you work with Otian AI, you work with us — the same two people, start to finish.

How We Work

Our Approach

Three things we hold to with every client, every time.

We Speak Human, Not Tech

We explain things in plain language. You’ll never get an email full of acronyms or jargon. If something technical comes up, we translate it.

We Only Set Up What You’ve Approved

Nothing gets connected or configured without your explicit sign-off. We walk through every access request with you before making any changes.

We Stay With You After Launch

Setup is not the end. It is the beginning. Ongoing support is included. If something needs adjusting or you want to add something new, just reach out.
