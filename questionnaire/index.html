Welcome!

What is an AI Agent, and what is OpenClaw?

Think of an AI agent as a personal assistant that lives on a small computer and works for you around the clock. Instead of opening an app and asking it questions one at a time, you can message it, just like texting a friend, and it goes and does the task: checks your email, organizes files, sets reminders, looks things up online, and more.

OpenClaw is the technology that makes this possible. It is the "engine" that runs your AI agent and connects it to apps you already use, like WhatsApp, email, or Telegram. We set this up and run it for you on a small dedicated computer (a Mac Mini), so you do not have to install or manage anything yourself.

A few things to know before we get started:

  • It is powered by AI models you may have heard of (like Claude or ChatGPT). These usually come with their own monthly subscription, separate from our setup service. We will help you figure out what fits your budget.
  • You are in control of what it can access. We will only connect it to the accounts and apps you approve, nothing more.
  • No tech experience needed. We handle the setup; you just tell us what you would like help with.
