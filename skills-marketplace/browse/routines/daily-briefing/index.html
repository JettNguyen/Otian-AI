Daily Briefing

by Otian AI v1.0.0 Free

Start every day informed. The Daily Briefing routine checks your Google Calendar each morning and sends a concise summary of the day's events to your Telegram chat. Includes meeting times, titles, and any preparation notes.

Requires: Calendar Management skill

Trigger Schedule

Runs daily at 08:00 UTC

Action

Prompts the Calendar Management skill: “Give me a complete summary of today’s calendar. Include event times, titles, locations, and any preparation I should do.” Delivered via Telegram notification.

Setup Steps

  1. Install the Calendar Management skill and connect Google Calendar first.
  2. Set your preferred briefing time (UTC).
