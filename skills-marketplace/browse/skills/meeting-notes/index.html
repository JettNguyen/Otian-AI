Meeting Notes

by Otian AI v1.4.0 $15 · one-time

Connect your Fireflies account and your agent quietly builds a memory of every meeting you have. It pulls new transcripts, works out which speaker is which person, writes a summary with the decisions and action items, and files it away. Then you can just ask: what have Sarah and I talked about recently? What did we decide about pricing? What did I commit to on Tuesday's call? When a transcript comes back with anonymous "Speaker 1" labels — which Fireflies does for uploads, Teams, and phone calls — the agent infers who's who from the transcript and the invite, shows you its guess, and remembers your correction for good.

Required Integrations

Fireflies

Trigger Keywords

meeting notes transcript meeting recap call notes action items recap what did we discuss what did we decide talked about discussed with who said last call catch me up follow-ups participants fireflies

Setup Steps

  1. Requires a Fireflies account — this skill reads the transcripts Fireflies records for you.
  2. In Fireflies, go to Settings → MCP & Dev Tools → Developer Settings and copy your API Key.
  3. Paste the key below, then set your timezone so meeting times display correctly.
  4. Ask the agent to sync your meetings, or install the Meeting Sync routine to keep it up to date automatically.

Configurable Variables

Variable Label Help
TIMEZONE Your timezone IANA timezone (e.g. America/Los_Angeles, Europe/London). Used to show meeting times.
DEFAULT_LOOKBACK_DAYS How far back "recent" means (days) When you ask about recent meetings without giving dates, the agent looks back this far.
MEETING_FOCUS What matters most in your meetings Shapes what the agent pulls out of a transcript.
