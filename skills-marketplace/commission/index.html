What It Can Be

Any of the Four Kinds

A commission can take the shape of any kind of add-on. If you’re not sure which one fits, don’t worry — that’s what scoping is for. New to add-ons? Start with the basics.

A Skill

Something new your agent can do — the app or tool it doesn’t know how to work with yet.

A Worker

A specialist your agent can hand work to — a researcher, a bookkeeper, a writer.

A Routine

Something it does on its own, on your schedule — the report you wish landed every Monday.

A Personality

How your agent talks to you — a voice and tone shaped around how you like to work.

Why We Work This Way
