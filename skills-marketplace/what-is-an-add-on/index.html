What's an Add-on?

The short version: it's anything you add to your agent to make it more yours.

The Idea

Your Agent, and Everything You Plug Into It

Archie arrives smart and capable on its own. Add-ons are how you shape it around your life — each one plugs in and stays there, quietly doing its part.

Why This Exists

You're Not Doing This Alone

Job searching. Meal prepping. Learning to budget. Figuring out a workout routine that actually sticks. Most people are working through the hard stuff solo, with no one really in their corner.

An add-on is a little bit of backup for exactly that — quietly watching the details, keeping track of what you'd otherwise forget, so you don't have to be "on" all the time. Just steady support for the parts of life nobody hands you a manual for.

The Four Kinds

Four Kinds of Add-on

"Add-on" is the umbrella. Underneath it are four kinds, and they answer four different questions: what can it do, how does it talk, when does it act, and who helps it.

Skills

Something new your agent can do — manage your calendar, sort your files, triage your inbox.

Personalities

How your agent talks to you. Warm and chatty, or brief and to the point.

Routines

Something it does on its own, on a schedule — like a summary of your day, every morning.

Workers

A specialist your agent can hand work to, like a researcher or a writer.

The Basics

Think of It Like Onboarding

Think of your agent like a new hire on their first day. They're smart and capable, but they don't yet know your tools, your accounts, or how you like things done.

An add-on is how your agent learns. It's a small piece that shows it how to work with something specific — your fitness app, your email, your calendar, your files.

Get Them Yourself, or Let Us

Browse the marketplace and pick one up yourself, or tell us what you want during a setup session and we'll handle it. Either way, there's nothing technical to configure.

Matched to How You Actually Work

Track workouts, manage your inbox, keep your files organized — whatever fits your day, there's likely an add-on for it.

Always in Your Control

You decide which add-ons your agent uses, and you can turn them on or off anytime.

In Practice

How It Actually Works

Say you use Hevy to track your workouts. Without an add-on, you'd open the app, tap through menus, and log everything by hand.

Without an Add-on

You open Hevy, tap through menus, and log every set by hand — sets, reps, weight, rest, repeat.

With an Add-on

You tell your agent, “I did 3 sets of squats at 135 lbs,” and it handles the rest. No app-switching, no manual entry.

That's the whole idea: one conversation, instead of five different apps.
