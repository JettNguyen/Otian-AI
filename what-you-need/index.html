What You Actually Need

Less than you’d think. Two things.

1

A computer you can leave on

Your agent answers while Archie is running, so you want a machine you don’t mind leaving on. Already have one? Use it. If it’s a laptop, it just needs to not fall asleep — we’ll sort that with you. Want a machine that’s always ready and never in your way? A Mac mini is the easy answer: inexpensive, silent, and happy to run around the clock.

Archie runs on Mac today. Windows is next — join the waitlist and we’ll tell you the day it lands.

2

An AI provider account

Anthropic or OpenAI, pay-as-you-go. That’s the thinking, and you only pay for what your agent actually does — a few dollars a month for a handful of tasks a day, more if you lean on it constantly. It’s billed straight to you, and you can cap it. We never touch it, and we never see it.

Archie itself is $20 a month when it ships — free while you’re a guided-setup client. Every cost, in full.

That’s the whole list.
